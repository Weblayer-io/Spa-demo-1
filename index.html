About Us

A Sanctuary of Wellness

Nestled in the heart of Navrangpura, Millennium Spa brings the ancient traditions of Turkish Hammam and Moroccan baths to Ahmedabad. Our expert therapists combine time-honored techniques with modern luxury to create an unforgettable wellness journey.

We are one of the few spas in Ahmedabad offering authentic Turkish and Moroccan Hammam rituals alongside modern luxury therapies. Our private luxury suites and specialized Couple's Rooms provide the perfect setting for shared relaxation.
